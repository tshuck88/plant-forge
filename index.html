PLANT FORGE VS STAKES


Training and canopy support with the Plant Forge is 4 times faster than traditional staking due to ergonomic design and functionality. The Plant Forge also trains more effectively, fills canopies more efficiently, and holds tops/branches in place more reliably than stakes. Reduce labor and get more effective results while keeping the advantages of a movable canopy with Plant Forge technology.

Stakes have a lot of built-in drawbacks:

  • Bamboo stakes are an organic material that can easily become host to pathogens such as mold spores, powdery mildew and even garden pests such as spider mites.
  • Stakes rot over time and can break easily, making them perishable garden equipment that needs to be replaced.
  • Staked gardens result in awkward and inefficient canopy arrangements reducing yields and diminishing spatial efficiency.
  • Staking plants is tedious and time consuming. Generally it takes 4 times longer per plant to properly support with stakes than it does to support with the Plant Forge.
  • Stakes damage plant roots and impact growth when they are driven into the rhizome.

Plant Forge technology solves every problem associated with staking while at the same time simply and effectively providing the same benefits and even improving on them. It is the clear choice for growers who need plant/canopy mobility but want a better option than stakes.

PLANT FORGE VS TRELLIS

With traditional trellis tech, plants become grouped together in a static canopy arrangement, often leaving plants in middle rows neglected, grown over, lacking optimal light and airflow. This can result in subpar quality and production from a large number of plants stuck within the midsections of trellis-rows.

The Plant Forge fixes these issues by enabling growers to move plants easily and access the interior of canopy spaces for inspections, group rotations, crucial workflows, and plant care. This flexibility lets growers give optimal attention to every plant in the garden and identify issues they may have otherwise not have found until it was too late. These advantages also contribute to maximizing quality and yield output of every plant in a garden space.

For those who demand high levels of care and the utmost plant quality and productivity, the Plant Forge provides a clear advantage to the traditional trellis method.
