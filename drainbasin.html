Drain Basin (Coming Soon)


The drain basin is designed to catch runoff water from feedings and simplify the flushing process. The basin has a 1.25 gallon holding capacity, ergonomic handles for easy transportation and pour-spouts for mess-free dumping. The drain basin fits any standard Plant Forge 4-gallon pot.
